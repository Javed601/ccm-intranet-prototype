About Marketing & Communications

The Marketing & Communications team is responsible for shaping and communicating the CCM Europe brand across all channels and markets. We develop strategies that connect our products and values with customers, partners, and employees.

Our work spans digital marketing, campaign management, corporate communications, trade events, media relations, and internal communications — ensuring a consistent, compelling voice for CCM Europe across Germany, Netherlands, UK, Romania and beyond.

We collaborate closely with Sales, Product Management, and the Executive team to align our messaging with business objectives and regional priorities.
